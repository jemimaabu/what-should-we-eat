FoodChooser
“ Hi, Welcome to the FoodChooser. May I take your order? ”

This app is designed to make choosing what to eat a lot easier, especially when you have a lot of options. It's also helpful for settling arguments when you want to eat one thing but somebody wants to eat something else.

How it works:
  • Add an option to the list of food
  • Eat it.
  • OR
  • Add a food choice from the suggested list below
  • Click the 'FoodChooser' button to select randomly from the options provided
